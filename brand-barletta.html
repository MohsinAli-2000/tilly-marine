Our Partnership

Tilly's Marine
& Barletta

Tilly's Marine has been Southern California's trusted Barletta dealer for years — and that relationship is built on a shared commitment to luxury, innovation, and the people who live for the water. As an authorized Barletta dealer serving Riverside, Woodland Hills, and Norco, we carry the full lineup of Barletta luxury pontoon boats.

What sets Barletta apart is what sets Tilly's Marine apart: an uncompromising standard. Every pontoon in our showroom is backed by the Barletta factory warranty, and our factory-certified technicians are trained to service every model we sell. When you buy a Barletta at Tilly's Marine, you're not just buying a pontoon — you're joining a community supported by one of the best dealer networks in the country.

Whether you're looking for the flagship Reserve Series, the family-friendly Cabrio Series, the versatile Lusso Series, or the performance-driven Corsa Series, our team has the expertise and inventory to match you with the right pontoon for your lifestyle, budget, and needs.
